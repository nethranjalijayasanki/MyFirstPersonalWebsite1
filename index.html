Connect 4 Game

Connect 4 is a two-player game played on a 6-row, 7-column grid. Players take turns dropping discs, aiming to connect four in a row, column, or diagonal. The grid is a 2D array, and discs fall to the lowest available spot in the chosen column. The game checks for a win or draw after each move and continues until there's a winner or the grid is full. Methods manage input, validate moves, and check for wins.
